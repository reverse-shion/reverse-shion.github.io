AMATO — Cosmetics Floor

Amber ceiling lamps soften every edge of the room, and rows of cosmetic counters stretch like polished altars beneath mirrored glass.

Powder, citrus, and the trace of white floral perfume drift together in the conditioned air, while brushes and palettes rest in silent geometric order.

Somewhere behind the reflections, Amato is present — not seen directly, only hinted by the measured movement of staff and the subtle recalibration of attention each time footsteps pass.
